Taking a look at the graph above you can see that most of the cities that experince 100% humidity is around the equator. Which is understandable because most of the rain forestes are locaated on the equator. Rain forestes are known of their tropical green climate. You already know when there is a lot of vegetation there must be a constant source of water either neither by or by rain fall. Considering that the closer you get to the equator the hotter it gets, in combinations with contant rain fall you can expect a high rate of humidity.
